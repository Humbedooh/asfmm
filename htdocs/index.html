Cheat Sheet / Help

  • You can keep messages off-the-record by prepending [off] to them.
  • You can mention (notify) someone by typing @asf-id in your message, where asf-id is their ASF userid, for instance @humbedooh.
  • You can make text italic with _text here_ or *text here*.
  • You can make text bold with __text here__ or **text here**.
  • Link highlighting is supported
